# Privacy Policy for Daxelo Kinrel

**Effective Date: March 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Daxelo Kinrel ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "Service").

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information when you use our Service:

- **Name**: Your display name as provided during registration or profile setup.
- **Date of Birth**: Your date of birth to enable birthday reminders and family milestone tracking.
- **Family Relations**: Information about your family members, including relationships, kinship terms, and family tree structure.
- **Photos**: Profile photos and family photos that you upload to the Service.
- **Device FCM Token**: Your device's Firebase Cloud Messaging token to send you push notifications.

We may also collect device information (device model, operating system, app version) and usage analytics to improve the Service.

## 3. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely using the following systems:

- **Cloudinary**: Photos and images you upload are stored on Cloudinary's secure cloud infrastructure.
- **PostgreSQL**: Your account information, family data, relationships, and application data are stored in our PostgreSQL database hosted on secure servers.
- **Isar (Local Storage)**: Certain data is cached locally on your device using Isar database for offline access and performance. This data remains on your device and is under your control.

All data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL. Passwords are stored as hashed values and are never stored in plain text.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate and improve the Service:

- **Firebase**: Used for push notifications (FCM), analytics, and authentication (Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In). Firebase is a service provided by Google LLC.
- **Cloudinary**: Used for image storage, transformation, and delivery of photos uploaded to the Service.
- **Razorpay**: Used for processing subscription payments securely. Razorpay handles all payment card information and we do not store your full card details.
- **Supabase**: Used for additional backend services, real-time features, and data synchronization.

Each third-party service provider has its own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review their policies. We only share the minimum information necessary for these services to function.

## 5. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain the Service
- Create and manage your family tree
- Send notifications about family events, birthdays, and milestones
- Process subscription payments
- Improve and personalize the Service
- Communicate with you about your account
- Ensure the security of the Service

## 6. Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data. To request data deletion:

1. Submit a data deletion request through the app, or
2. Send an email to **privacy@daxelo.com** with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"

We will process your data deletion request within **30 days** of receipt. Upon completion, all your personal data, family contributions, and associated records will be permanently deleted from our systems, except where retention is required by law.

## 7. Data Sharing

**We do not sell your personal data to third parties.**

We only share your data in the following circumstances:

- With your explicit consent
- With family members within your family tree as part of the Service functionality
- With our third-party service providers as described in Section 4
- When required by law or to protect our legal rights

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

Parents or guardians may create family entries for minors within their family tree, and parental consent mechanisms are available for data processing related to children.

## 9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

- Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Hashed password storage
- Regular security audits
- Access controls on our infrastructure

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

- Right to access your personal data
- Right to correct inaccurate data
- Right to delete your data
- Right to data portability
- Right to withdraw consent
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
