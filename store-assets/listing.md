## Full Description
Daxelo Kinrel helps Indian families map and understand
their relationships in 14 languages including Hindi,
Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Bengali,
Gujarati, Punjabi, Odia, Assamese, Urdu, Sanskrit,
and English.

Features:
• Interactive family tree with 500+ member support
• Kinship names in 14 Indian languages
• AI-powered relationship discovery
• Voice notes for family stories
• Birthday and event reminders
• Migration map showing family origins
• Offline support — works without internet
• Share family tree with QR codes
